A staple in every Polish Kitchen

Description:

This mexican dish has been an immensely popular choice in the United States for a long time. It's also quite diverse with the meat an toppings options, but for this recipe we will focus on a ground beef quesadilla and a very basic recipe.

The Quesadilla Ingredients

Quesadilla

  • 1 large tortilla
  • 1/2 a white or yellow onion
  • 2 jalapeno peppers
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • 200 grams grated monterey jack cheese
  • 100 grams grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

    • Sauce

  • 30 grams finely chopped cilantro
  • 60 grams sour cream
  • 60 grams salsa verde

    • Steps

    1. Heat a medium non-stick sauce pan on medium heat.
    2. Add your 500g of ground beef and cook for 5-10 minutes or until browned.
    3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
    4. Add one chopped onion, then add two sliced jalapenos and finely sliced garlic.
    5. Turn heat up to medium high and cook until everything has softened.
    6. Remove the meat filling mixture out of the pan into a medium sized bowl.
    7. At this point, mix your ingredients for the sauce in a cup or small bowl to use later.
    8. Mix your grated cheese into a small bowl together as well.
    9. With a clean pan, heat to medium heat and add 1 tbsp butter.
    10. When melted, add your tortilla, then add a desired amount of your sauce to one half of the tortilla.
    11. Add half your cheese mixture on top of your sauce.
    12. Add 50 grams of your meat mixture on the cheese, then cover with the other half of the cheese mix.
    13. Fold your tortilla over and cook on that side an additional 2 minutes.
    14. Reduce heat to medium low.
    15. Flip your tortilla and cook an additional 5 minutes.
    16. After all the cheese has melted and the tortilla has turned a golden brown color on both sides, remove from heat and serve with the sauce on the side.
    17. And most importantly, enjoy!
